# Basic controls
WASD: Move around
Left Mouse Button: Use Handcannon
Right Mouse Button: Does nothing
Space: Jump

ALT: Unlock Mouse

# Movement Mechanics

## Bunny Hopping
Hold down space to automatically bunnyhop, allowing you to maintain horizontal speed for much longer.

## Air strafing
While airborne, turn slowly to redirect your momentum in the desired direction.
NOTE: Turning too quickly will cause you to lose speed.
NOTE 2: Unlike in Quake, this does not build speed.

## Blast Jumping
Fire a rocket nearby to launch yourself in the other direction. If the explosion is directly below you it will yield a stronger vertical boost.

## Trimping
While going very fast, you can launch yourself off a slope, redirecting your momentum upwards.
NOTE: Bunny hopping will override trimping.
